Schedule

As a member of the Order of the Arrow, I will:

  • Observe the Scout Oath, Scout Law, and the Order of the Arrow Obligation.
  • Wear my official Class A uniform when appropriate and Conclave identification throughout Conclave.
  • Attend activities and training opportunities.
  • Be personally responsible for breakage, damage, or loss of property.
  • Not smoke inside any buildings and be advised of smoking regulations regarding adult smoking. No smoking is permitted under 18 years of age.
  • Respect all rules regarding vehicles in camp.
  • Not leave camp property without receiving proper permission.
  • Understand that the possession or consumption of alcoholic beverages or illegal drugs at Conclave will not be permitted. Compliance with state laws and camp regulations will apply at all times.
  • At all times, be the considerate guest of a considerate camp host.
